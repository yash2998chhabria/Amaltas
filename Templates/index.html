Our Concept

Amaltas' E-Exhibition is an online shopping experience that helps customers connect with talented and reliable entrepreneurs who sell exquisite, high-quality products all over India. Our products range from clothes, accessories, festive special items such as Rakhi, handicrafts and more!.

After successfully organising more than 20 physical exhibitions over the past 7 years, Amaltas has built this one of a kind online exhibition to keep our loyal customers and stall owners happy and adapt to the circumstances we live in today
